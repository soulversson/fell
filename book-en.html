Apply for space

Fell Residency is open from January 10 - May 31 and from August 15 to December 15.

The duration of each stay is at minimum one week and 12 weeks at the most.

The rent price for a one-week stay is as follows:
A single room (for one person): 30.000 ISK or 220 EUR
A double room (for two people): 45.000 ISK or 325 EUR

Period is in weeks and there are arrival and departure days on Fridays.
Check-in at 17:00
Departure at 12:00

Included in the price are towels and bedclothes, sanitary products, WiFi and access to communal spaces.

Guests take care of their own washing and cleaning.

We will never share your data
